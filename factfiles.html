Carolina Reaper

The Carolina Reaper is the hottest chilli in the world!
The Carolina Reaper averages one and a half million scoviles but can reach nearly double that.

It's main features are:

  1. Red and gnarled skin

  2. A pointy tail

  3. bright red

Bhut Jolokia

One of the hottest spices,
this amazing chilli has a scoville rating of one million.
It was once the hottest chilli in the world!

It's main features are:

  1. bright red

  2. lumpy texture

  3. quite long but not very thin
